Watching Heated Rivalry for New Years

It’s strange to notice yourself changing. My screen time was cut in half this year, I’m more politically motivated than ever, but less angry because I understand hate won’t fix anything. I’ve been moving spontaneously. Even the way I text is different. Ashley was the most punk rock person I know— I think that’s given me confidence. Anytime I’m scared, she motivates me to keep moving.
